About

A free community resource for the Greater Milwaukee area — built to lower the barriers between deciding to get help and getting it.

Why this exists

Reaching out is hard, and waiting for an appointment is harder. This site is a free, credible place to start right away — practical coping, plain-language education, and where to find care locally — and a link anyone can share with someone who needs it.

Who's behind it

MKE Mental Health is an independent community resource for Greater Milwaukee — not affiliated with any single clinic. Content is reviewed by licensed clinicians.

What this is — and isn't

  • It is free education and a resource directory.
  • It is not therapy, medical advice, or a crisis service.
  • It does not collect personal health information.

For workplaces & leaders

Leaders increasingly need a way to support employee mental health and reduce stigma — without becoming therapists. This is a credible, free link you can share with your team or group, and a starting point for healthier conversations at work.
