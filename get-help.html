Get help now

If you're struggling right now, you're not alone — here's where to turn immediately.

If it's an emergency

  • Thoughts of suicide or in crisis: call or text 988 — the Suicide & Crisis Lifeline, free and confidential, 24/7.
  • Immediate medical danger: call 911 or go to the nearest emergency room.
  • Milwaukee County Crisis Line: [confirm number before launch] — local crisis support and mobile crisis response.
Not sure if it's an emergency? If you're thinking about harming yourself or someone else, or you can't stay safe right now, treat it as one — call or text 988.

Need to talk, but it's not an emergency?

The NAMI HelpLine offers free information and support — call 1-800-950-6264 or text "NAMI" to 62640 (Mon–Fri). It is a support and information line, not a crisis line; for crisis, use 988.

Take a quick self-check

A short, anonymous mental-health screening can help you understand what you're experiencing and what kind of support might help. Mental Health America offers free, confidential screenings.

Take a free screening (MHA)

A screening is not a diagnosis. It's a starting point — results can help you decide whether to reach out for care. If results worry you or you're in crisis, call or text 988.
